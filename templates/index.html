Hello, Welcome to the City-Data app.

This app may not look super pretty, but is sure is informative.

Enter in a bunch of cities to find out the current temp and a bunch of information scraped from wikipedia.

Long batches of cities will take longer to look up. Works best for American cities.

A graph displaying population density will be shown and a link do download the information

will be at the bottom of the page.

AutoSuggestions for ~1000 most populated US cities from: https://simplemaps.com/data/us-cities

Temperature information from openweathermap.org API

All other information comes from scraping wikipedia
